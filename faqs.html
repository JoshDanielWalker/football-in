FAQs

Find the answer to your questions.

    • Q1 - I'd like to go ahead - what do I need to do now?

      A1 - Please submit the application form or speak to one of our team to take these details over the phone, we will then discuss the details of what table you would like and arrange a date for delivery.

    • Q2 - Is there a long contract involved or any hidden costs?

      A2 - No, we only ask that you agree to take the table for a minimum of 3 months.

    • Q3 - How often will the table be emptied?

      A3 - We will visit approximately once every 6 – 8 weeks to empty and service the table.

    • Q4 - How are the takings shared out?

      A4 - The profits are simply split 50/50.

    • Q5 - How much does it cost per play?

      A5 - £1.00 per game, however 50p play can be arranged on request.

    • Q6 - Do you have any tables for sale?

      A6 - Yes, as well as new tables, we do usually have ex-rental tables available to buy, please contact us for availability and current prices.
